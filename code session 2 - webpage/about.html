About

Durham City

A county in North-east England, a city with many historic stories that dates to as early as the year 1072 when Durham Castle was built after the Norman invasion (Parkes, 2022). Filled with histories including the legend of the Dun Cow and the milkmaid in the year 995AD, two of Christianity’s most important saints, Battle of Neville’s Cross in 1346, and more (Johnson, n.d.). And a city that homes to the University of Durham, one of the few UK universities with a collegiate system (Johnson, n.d.).

Our purpose

This website aims to let people from bustling busy cities switch off their office-life and have a getaway weekend at Durham where they can forget about the life of sitting by computers for hours. Be surrounded by cafes and an environment where it will make you feel like you're in a Harry Potter movie (2 Harry Potter movies were shot in Durham).

Two of the Harry Potter movies were filmed in Durham Cathedral: In the Cloister Garth, and in the Cloister hallway. Go ahead and have a look!
